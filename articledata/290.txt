Driverless cars are dodging pedestrians and pheasants in Oxford
Our reporters view inside the driverless car
Sam Wong
By Sam Wong
Its a Ford Mondeo, but not as we know it. This one has about 100,000 worth of sensors, cameras on the roof, and a fairly ordinary computer in the boot, enabling it to drive itself . So far its notched up hundreds of miles on public roads in Oxford and the surrounding area. By September, it will be one of a fleet of six autonomous cars driving to London.
