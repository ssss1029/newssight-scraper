India should be most populous country in 2024
India should be most populous country in 2024
brian wang | April 20, 2018 |

India is projected to surpass Chinas population in 2024.
In 2024, India and China are expected to have roughly a population of 1.44 billion each. After that, Indias population is projected to continue growing for several decades to around 1.5 billion in 2030 and approaching 1.66 billion in 2050, while the population of China is projected to remain stable until the 2030s, after which it may begin a slow decline.
